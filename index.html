Melinda's Seafood Restaurant - Santa Clara

Established in 1991, Melinda's Seafood Restaurant is Santa Clara’s choice for seafood. Located at the Westfield Valley Fair, Melinda's Seafood Restaurant features a large selection of live oysters, seasonal catches, sushi and live shellfish, including lobster and crab, on a menu that changes daily.

Happy Lunar New Year

Celebrate the Year of the Snake at Melinda's Seafood Restaurant. We usher in the Lunar New Year with seasonal decor and special dishes to help families celebrate this time of year, including .Garlic Noodles, Jumbo Lump Whole Crispy Black Sea Bass and Wok Fried Spiny Lobster.

Reserve Surf & Turf this Valentine's Day

Whether you're raising a glass to romance, friendship, or just celebrating your love of stellar food and wine, our Valentine's Day menus pair world-class flavors with unparalleled service for an evening beyond compare. Choose from an 8oz Filet and Lobster Tail For One, or a shareable 16oz Chateaubriand and 12oz Lobster Tail For Two — paired with Seared Scallops With Risotto to start and a luxurious Dark Chocolate Luxardo Cherry Tart as decadent as the evening itself. Available Thursday, February 13 – Monday, February 17.
