What's New

Spiny Lobster

California Spiny Lobsters are found from Morro Bay down to Baja California. We work with local lobstermen to catch them by traps and by hand, one at a time. From a conservation standpoint, it’s one of the most regulated fisheries in North America. We’re excited to offer you this unique California seafood experience each season – it’s arguably the best seafood product in the world.

Dungeness Crab

A unique West Coast seafood experience, the Dungeness Crab are known for their sweet and delectable meat. Pull up a chair and get those crab crackers ready to enjoy these delicious crustaceans!

Halibut

The world's largest species of flatfish is here, all the way from the central Gulf of Alaska! We offer several preparations during the season, including pan-seared, grilled and Macadamia nut-crusted Halibut.

Hot Fish Taco

Golden, crispy Alaska cod, delicately fried to perfection, serves as the heart of this vibrant dish. A bold, spicy sambal glaze coats each piece, adding a tantalizing kick that lingers with every bite. Beneath the crisp exterior lies a refreshing crunch of shredded cabbage, mingling harmoniously with tangy pickled carrots, fragrant cilantro, and thinly sliced jalapeños for a burst of freshness. On the side, a sesame slaw offers a crisp, nutty contrast, rounding out this beautifully layered, flavor-packed creation.
